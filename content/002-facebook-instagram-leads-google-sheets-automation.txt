Workflow Steps: How to Connect Facebook Lead Ads to Google Sheets
1. Create a free Make.com account — no credit card required. Click "Create a new scenario" on your dashboard.
2. Click the large purple + button on the canvas and search for "Facebook Lead Ads" — select "Watch Leads" as your trigger

Image: assets/screenshots/screenshot-facebook-leads-2.png|Facebook Lead Ads module — click Create a connection to link your Facebook account

3. Click "Create a connection", name it anything (e.g. "My Facebook connection"), click Save — Facebook OAuth will open automatically
4. Once connected, select your Facebook Page and the Lead Ad Form you want to watch

Image: assets/screenshots/screenshot-facebook-leads-3.png|Facebook Lead Ads configuration — Connection and Page selection

5. Click the + button on the right of the Facebook module to add a second module — search for "Google Sheets" and select "Add a Row"
6. Connect your Google account and select the spreadsheet where leads should land — choose "Search by path" and select your file

Image: assets/screenshots/screenshot-facebook-leads-4.png|Google Sheets module connected to Facebook Lead Ads in the scenario canvas

7. Map the fields from Facebook to your Sheet columns — drag Full name to Name column, Email to Email column, Phone number to Phone column, Ad name to Source column, Date created to Date column

Image: assets/screenshots/screenshot-facebook-leads-5.png|Field mapping — Facebook lead data mapped to Google Sheets columns

8. Click Save — Make.com will ask "From which point do you want to start?" — select "From now on" to capture only new leads going forward
9. Click "Run once" to activate, then turn the scenario ON — the toggle at the bottom of the canvas

Image: assets/screenshots/screenshot-facebook-leads-7.png|Completed Google Sheet with live lead data — Name, Email, Phone, Source and Date columns populated automatically

FAQ:
Q: Does this work with Instagram lead ads too?
A: Yes — Facebook and Instagram lead ads share the same Meta Ads infrastructure. The Facebook Lead Ads module in Make.com captures leads from both platforms automatically.
Q: What if someone submits a lead while Make.com is not running?
A: Make.com checks for new leads on a schedule — every 15 minutes on the free plan, in real time on paid plans. Leads submitted between checks are queued and processed on the next run, nothing is lost.
Q: Do I need a developer to set this up?
A: No. Make.com's visual drag-and-drop builder requires no coding. If you can use Google Sheets, you can build this automation.
Q: What does "From now on" mean when setting up the trigger?
A: Make.com asks from which point to start processing leads. "From now on" means only new leads submitted after you activate the scenario will be captured. Choose "All" only if you want to import existing leads retroactively.
